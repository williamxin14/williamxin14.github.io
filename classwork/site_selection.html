Site Selection Analysis

Introduction

Final Project for GIS Spatial Analysis class

planned a biological reserve system within Centre County PA by identifying lands within the county defined by experts that meet the criteria for conservation efforts. The Criteria for suitable land use of conservation is listed below

  • Greater than 70 bird and mammalian species combined
  • Less than 10% of each study area occupied by buffered roads
  • High habitat potential
  • Public owned land
  • Forested areas
  • Slope less than 10%

Criteria Implementation

After all six Criteria has been spatially realized, they are converted into binary layers (suitable/unsuitable)
