Emergency Relief Zones

Introduction

Assignment for GIS Spatial Analysis class

This assignment was designed to teach students how to use GIS for Emergency/Disaster Managemment. The assighment uses a real life scenerio, the 2011 super torandoes that struck across Alabama which was one of the deadliest attacks. Site selection Analysis is used to find suitbale locations for emergency relief sites.

The Relief Zone is calucated by using buffer analysis. As shown in the image below the zone should be outside the tornado's path of destruction but close enough to help those in need of assistence. The size and shape of the relief zone is determined the strength of toronado. Relief Priority is calucated based on density and tornado intensity.
